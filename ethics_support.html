Dissertation Process

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth dissertation process.

  • Submit Project Idea: Students can submit their project ideas for initial review and ethical considerations.This feature allows students to submit their project or dissertation idea to their assigned supervisors or review committee for evaluation and approval.
  • Topic Recommendation: Based on the submitted idea, students receive recommendations to refine their research topic.Helps students select a dissertation topic when they are unsure or need guidance. Supervisors or the system itself can recommend topics aligning with academic goals or supervisor expertise.
  • Meeting with Guide: Students can schedule a meeting with their guide using Google Meet for further discussion and guidance.Scheduling and conducting meetings with the assigned guide (supervisor) to discuss research progress, challenges, and future tasks.
  • Upload Document: Approved documents can be uploaded for review, ensuring compliance with dissertation guidelines. Allows students to upload dissertation-related documents at various stages (e.g., proposal, draft versions, final submission).
  • Feedback: Guides provide feedback on submitted documents, helping students refine their research.Facilitates supervisors to provide structured feedback on dissertation drafts, meeting agendas, or research progress.
