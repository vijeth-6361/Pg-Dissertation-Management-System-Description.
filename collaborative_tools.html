Ensuring Ethical Research Practices

Our platform provides guidance and resources to help you navigate ethical compliance in research.

  • Social and Clinical Value: Research should provide valuable knowledge to benefit society and clinical practice, ensuring its relevance and significance.
  • Scientific Validity: Studies must follow rigorous methodologies, ensuring reliability, replicability, and accuracy in findings.
  • Fair Course Selection: Researchers should ensure participant selection is justifiable, unbiased, and scientifically appropriate.
  • Favorable Risk-Benefit Ratio: Risks should be minimized while ensuring that potential benefits justify the study.
  • Independent Review: Ethical oversight committees should evaluate research proposals to ensure compliance with regulations.
  • Respect for Potential and Enrolled Courses: Participants must be treated ethically, with confidentiality and their rights respected throughout the study.
  • Profile Creation: Researchers should create a professional profile to document compliance and track ethical approvals.
  • Submit Project Idea: Research proposals should be submitted with thorough ethical considerations for review.
  • Topic Recommendation: Researchers should receive guidance on selecting ethical and impactful research topics.
  • Meeting with Guide: Regular mentorship sessions should be conducted to ensure adherence to ethical standards.
  • Upload Document: Researchers should securely store and submit ethical approval documents and progress reports.
  • Continuous Ethical Monitoring: Regular audits and self-assessments should be performed to ensure ongoing compliance with ethical guidelines.
  • Data Security & Confidentiality: Sensitive research data should be protected against breaches, ensuring privacy and ethical handling.
