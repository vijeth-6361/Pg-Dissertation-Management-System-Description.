Access to Research Resources

Explore top-tier research platforms to enhance your dissertation work.

  • Google ScholarGoogle Scholar provides a simple way to search for scholarly literature across disciplines and sources, including theses, books, and conference papers.
  • JSTORJSTOR offers access to thousands of academic journals, primary sources, and books across multiple disciplines.
  • ScienceDirectScienceDirect is a premier database offering high-quality scientific, technical, and medical research articles.
  • PubMedPubMed is a free resource for searching biomedical and life sciences literature with citations and abstracts.
  • SpringerSpringer provides research articles, books, and conference papers covering numerous scientific fields.
  • ResearchGateResearchGate is a networking platform for researchers to share publications, collaborate, and seek feedback.
  • Academia.eduAcademia.edu allows academics to share their research papers and connect with peers globally.
  • ProQuestProQuest provides access to a wide array of dissertations, theses, and academic research materials.
  • DOABThe Directory of Open Access Books offers freely available academic books from various disciplines.
  • Taylor & FrancisTaylor & Francis Online provides a vast library of peer-reviewed journal articles and books.
